# Top 7 CIO Priorities

1.	Align to business functionality
2.	Achieve the agility that enterprise needs
3.	Mitigate risk
4.	Raise performance levels
5.	Stay ahead of the curve
6.	Find right balance between cost, quality, and speed
7.	Lower TCO
